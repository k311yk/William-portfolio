About





I am a 2nd year PhD candidate for the Safe AI Lab at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Ding Zhao. I received my BA in Psychological Science at the University of California, Irvine.


My research interest is broadly in multimodal learning, particularly with physiological signals, for applications in cognitive science, embodied artificial intelligence (AI), and healthcare. I work with vision and language as well. More recently, I am super interested in multimodal and representation learning with electrocardiograms (ECGs) and text.
